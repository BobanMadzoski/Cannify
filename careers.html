Do you share our mission?

Cannify Pharma GmbH is a fully licensed wholesaler for medicinal cannabis. Our cannabinoid drugs alleviate the symptoms of chronically ill people and improve their quality of life. We want more and more patients in Germany to have safe and regulated access to high-quality cannabis medicines - we need your help!

As a rapidly growing company, we are always looking for committed and ambitious employees who support our mission. If you too want to revolutionize the market for cannabis products, apply to Cannify!
